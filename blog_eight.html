With our wireframes, we wanted to use a grid system with all of our elements inside of it. By using a grid system for our wireframes, it would be easier to make a responsive site for all devices. Inside of this grid, we would contain elements such as our filter content, articles videos etc, and our footer.

We wanted to give all of our pages the same look as we wanted to keep the consistency throughout each page.

For our filter content section, we originally had it overlap some of the content on the page, however, we decided to give the filter its own section. This was a design decision from the group which works well as the filter section takes up a lot of space on the page upon click.
