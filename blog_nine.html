During feedback in class, we were given heaps of feedback from other groups on how we could improve our prototypes.

Originally for our mobie version, we had our navigation bar cover only half of the page, however getting feedback from one of the groups, they mentioned that it would be better for it to cover the whole page.

For our submit content page, it was lacking the consistency that other pages had. This meant that we needed to change the colours we used to make the page belong on the website.

From stakeholder feedback, we needed to change the video layout page as it didn't harmonize well with the other pages.
